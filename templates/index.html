Introduction

This site is designed so that you can easily use the controllers i made using mediapipe and OpenCv on any device from anywhere at anytime.
I have designed three types of controller : Mouse, Media and Racing game.

Mouse Pointer

  • Move Pointer

  • Click

  • Scroll Up

  • Scroll Down

Racing Game Controller

  • Go Straight

  • Turn Left

  • Turn Right

  • Brakes Applied

Media Player

  • Play/Pause

  • Volume Up

  • Volume Down

  • Forward

  • Backward
